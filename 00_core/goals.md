# Goals

## Career Direction

- My long-term goal is to join a top-tier or international company through continuous skill development and capability building.
- I aim to increase my overall market competitiveness and transition into higher-impact engineering roles over time.
---

## Strategic Focus (Current Priorities)

- Due to financial constraints, I need to remain employed while pursuing long-term goals.
- My immediate priority is to stabilize performance in my current role by ramping up quickly and gaining control over daily work.
- Achieving a stable and manageable working state will allow me to reserve energy and focus for long-term development.
- I aim to gradually shift from short-term survival mode to a more structured growth-oriented routine.

---

## Short-term (0-3 months)

- Quickly ramp up in the current role and reduce uncertainty
- Reach a controlled and predictable working state (avoid being overwhelmed)
- Establish consistent English learning habits (especially listening and speaking)

---

## Mid-term (3-12 months)

- Evaluate long-term fit within the current company
- After ~1 year:
  - Actively maintain and update resume
  - Build and refine portfolio/projects aligned with career direction

---

## Long-term (1-3 years)

- Join a top-tier or international company
- Transition into higher-impact engineering roles
